Our Mission

Breaking language barriers across Africa

Millions of Africans speak languages that the world's major translation tools ignore. Kasa AI is changing that — one language at a time, starting right here in Ghana.

"Kasa" means speak or language in Akan (Twi). It's the perfect name for a tool built to give African languages a voice in the age of AI.

We combine cutting-edge AI with deep cultural knowledge to build translations that don't just convert words — they carry meaning, context, and community.

Supported Languages

Starting with Ghana's voices

Our first release focuses on the languages that define everyday life in Ghana, with a growing roadmap across the continent.

🌟

Akan

Twi · Fante

Ghana's most widely spoken indigenous language, with over 9 million speakers. Akan encompasses Twi and Fante dialects across the Ashanti, Eastern, and Central regions.

"Ɛte sɛn?" — How are you?
🌿

Ga

Greater Accra

The language of the Ga people and the heart of Accra, Ghana's capital. Ga is spoken by over 700,000 people and carries centuries of coastal Ghanaian heritage.

"Te oyiwaladon?" — How are you?
🗣️

English

Official Language

Ghana's official language and the bridge to global communication. Our translations preserve the richness of Ghanaian English alongside formal and informal registers.

"Akwaaba" — Welcome (Akan loanword widely used)
